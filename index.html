✨ Features

Your desktop, more delightful with it

Desktop Hachimi brings cute anime characters to your desktop, keeping you company while you work, study, or just relax.

🐾
Animated Pet Sprite
Supports multi-frame GIF animations. Your pet walks and idles on screen — full of life.
🖱️
Mouse Follow Mode
Enable it and your pet locks onto your cursor, never leaving your side.
🎵
Built-in Music Player
Add local tracks and enjoy music while you pet your companion.
🎨
Highly Customizable
Adjust size, opacity, and speed. Edit animation weights and flip direction for a truly unique pet.
🏠
Pet Creation Workshop
Import your own GIF assets with the built-in creator to make a one-of-a-kind sprite.
🌐
Multi-language Support
Ships with Chinese, English, and more locales — ready to use out of the box.
📌
Always on Top
Stays visible above all windows, no matter what app you're using.
🚀
Launch at Startup
Auto-starts with your system so your pet is always there to greet you.
🪪
Multi-pet Management
Manage multiple pet characters at once — switch, add, or remove them freely.
📦 Installation

Get started in a few steps

No complex setup required — just download and run.

1
Download the latest Release
Head to GitHub Releases and download the latest installer or executable.
2
Extract / Run
Windows users: double-click the .exe file. If you downloaded an archive, extract it first.
3
Configure via Tray Menu
After launch the pet appears on your desktop. Right-click it to open the menu and adjust size, speed, pets, and more.
🎉
Enjoy your new desktop buddy!
Enable mouse follow, open the music player, or import your own GIF to create a custom character.
